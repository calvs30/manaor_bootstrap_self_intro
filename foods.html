A Taste of My Favorites

Flavors I love, dishes I crave, and meals that bring comfort and joy. Each bite tells a story of taste, memories, and moments worth savoring.

Main Dishes

Sinigang

Sinigang is my favorite because of its comforting sour broth that instantly warms me up. I love how the balance of flavors makes every spoonful refreshing.

Adobo

Adobo is special to me because of its rich, savory taste and tender meat. I like it since it feels familiar and comforting, especially when paired with rice.

Ginisang Ampalaya

Ginisang Ampalaya is something I enjoy because of its unique bitter flavor that grows on you. I like it because it feels healthy and simple, yet still very flavorful.

Desserts

Leche Plan

Leche Flan is something I really like because of its silky texture and rich sweetness. I enjoy it because the caramel flavor makes it feel indulgent and comforting.

Halo-halo

Halo-halo is something I love because it’s refreshing and full of different textures and flavors. I enjoy it especially on hot days since it’s colorful, fun, and perfectly sweet.

Ube Halaya

Ube Halaya is special to me because of its smooth, creamy texture and deep ube flavor. I like it because it has a unique taste that stands out and uniquely Filipino..
